Francis Christy Boy
Chief Executive Officer
Rebus Holdings
Francis Christy Boy is a visionary executive with over 30 years of experience in engineering, construction, project management, and construction software development across the UAE and GCC. Starting his entrepreneurial path in 1992, Francis first founded an education and technology venture before establishing Engineering & Contracting in 1997.
Founded in 1996 — Today we serve a handful of customers with a broad range of products and services that power shopping, business, and cloud computing.

Francis has played a pivotal role in executing most of the UAE’s iconic projects, which include:

  • Dubai International Airport
  • Zayed International Airports
  • The Dubai Mall
  • Abu Dhabi World Trade Centre
  • Barakah Nuclear Power Plant
  • Emirates Towers
  • ADNOC projects in Ruwais, UAE
  • Etisalat Towers

Building upon this track record, Francis incorporated Rebus Project Management, UAE in 2016 to specialize in project closeout and consultancy, Rebus Project Management, UK in 2023, Rebus Information Technology Company, KSA in 2024, Rebus Technology Solutions LLC, UAE in 2025 and Rebus Holdings Company LLC in 2025 focusing on next-generation services, software and solutions for construction management.

A pivotal achievement in Francis’s career is the creation of CloseoutSoft, an internally developed construction management solution crafted to streamline and optimize project closeout processes and operational readiness. Introduced over nine years ago using Dot Net technology, this software has been central to Rebus’s ability to deliver projects with 100% on-time completion. In February 2025, Francis embarked on a major redevelopment of CloseoutSoft utilizing a modern technology stack, significantly enhancing its scalability and flexibility. This upgraded platform is designed to support businesses ranging from small teams to enterprises with over 100,000 users. Scheduled for completion by November 2025, the next-generation CloseOutSoft is set to empower both Rebus’s existing clients and other companies with advanced, comprehensive construction management capabilities.

Deeply grateful for the UAE’s role in his professional journey, Francis is dedicated to giving back by revolutionizing project delivery through innovative, accessible technology. Guided by the tagline, “We complete the incomplete,” Francis leads Rebus Holdings in setting new standards for construction management and closeout solutions in the region.
